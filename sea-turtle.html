About Sea Turtles

Sea turtles are marine reptiles that inhabit oceans worldwide. They play an important role in maintaining healthy seagrass beds and coral reefs.

Habitat

They can be found in both temperate and tropical waters, often migrating long distances to find food and nesting sites.

Diet

Sea turtles primarily feed on seagrasses, algae, and jellyfish, depending on the species.

Conservation Status

Many species of sea turtles are endangered due to habitat loss, climate change, and poaching.
